💎 Why ArtSoul?
✨
🎯 Vision
ArtSoul is a revolutionary platform for decentralized art, where value is determined by the community, not by galleries or auction houses.

We create a space where every artist can present their work to the world, and the community decides its true value through a transparent auction process.
🔍
❓ Problem
Traditional art market:
• Closed to most artists
• Prices set by galleries and critics
• High commissions (30-50%)
• No transparency in pricing
• Difficult access for collectors

Web3 solutions often:
• Too technical for regular users
• Focus only on NFTs
• Ignore real art
• Lack user-friendly UX
🚀
💡 Solution
ArtSoul offers:

1. **Democratized Pricing**
- Community votes with bids
- Transparent price formation
- No purchase obligation when bidding

2. **Multi-format Support**
- NFT, video, music, GIF, photos
- Real art (tokenized)
- All in one place

3. **Easy to Use**
- Two modes: Brutal (classic) and Future (Web3)
- Intuitive interface
- Drag & drop upload

4. **Multi-chain Support**
- Ethereum, Polygon, BSC, Solana, Xion
- Any wallet
- Low fees
🔧
⚙️ How It Works
**For Artists:**
1. Create profile
2. Upload your work
3. Set your estimate (optional)
4. Community places bids
5. Floor price is formed
6. Work goes to auction

**For Collectors:**
1. Connect wallet
2. Browse gallery
3. Bid on works you like
4. Your bid influences floor price
5. Buy at market price

**Pricing:**
- Artist sets their estimate
- Community places bids
- Highest bid = floor price
- Not a purchase obligation
- It's a vote for value
