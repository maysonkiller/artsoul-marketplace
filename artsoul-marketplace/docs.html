Getting Started

ArtSoul is a decentralized art marketplace where community determines value. Connect your wallet to start exploring, bidding, and uploading artwork.

Triple Valuation System

Every artwork has three values: Creator Value (set by artist), Community Value (average from voters), and System Value (AI-based on 3-day auction data).

Uploading Artwork

Connect your wallet, complete your profile, then navigate to Upload. Supported formats: Images, Videos, Music, GIFs. Set your initial price and description.

Bidding & Auctions

Place bids on artworks you love. The 3-day auction system ensures fair pricing. Highest bidder wins when auction ends.

Supported Networks

Ethereum, Polygon, BSC, Base, Arbitrum, Optimism, Sepolia, Rialo Playground. Switch networks anytime via the wallet modal.
